WMRN Broadcast Timeline

1940

December 23: WMRN-AM signs on at 1500 kilocycles. First broadcast at 1:15 p.m.

1941

AM frequency moves to 1490 kHz under NARBA realignment.

1953

May 6: WMRN-FM launches at 106.9 MHz (Channel 295).

2008

January 6: WMRN-FM frequency changes to 94.3 MHz.

Present

AM (1490 kHz) and FM broadcast history documented and preserved by the WMRN Radio History Archive.

Institutional Statement

The WMRN Radio History Archive exists to preserve, document, and provide structured access to the broadcast legacy of WMRN-AM (1490) and WMRN-FM in Marion, Ohio. Materials are cataloged, described, and presented in accordance with archival standards and historical documentation practices.
