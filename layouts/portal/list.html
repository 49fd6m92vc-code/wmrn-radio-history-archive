Institutional Statement

The WMRN Radio History Archive exists to preserve, document, and provide structured access to the broadcast legacy of WMRN-AM and WMRN-FM in Marion, Ohio. Materials are cataloged, described, and presented in accordance with archival standards and historical documentation practices.
