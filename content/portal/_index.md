## Preservation Projects

The archive maintains ongoing preservation initiatives including:

- Digitization of historic broadcasts
- Documentation of early AM and FM licensing records
- Historical building and studio research
- Oral history preservation

---

## Institutional Statement

The WMRN Radio History Archive is an independent historical preservation initiative dedicated to documenting and preserving the broadcast legacy of WMRN.

Materials are cataloged, described, and presented for historical, educational, and research purposes.
