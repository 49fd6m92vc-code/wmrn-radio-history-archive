# THE WMRN ARCHIVES: “Power Out; SOS” - Emergency Coverage Promo, WMRN News

This episode presents a preserved WMRN News promotional announcement titled “Power Out; SOS.”

Framed with urgency and immediacy, the promo reinforces WMRN’s identity as a dependable lifeline during power outages and emergency situations affecting Marion, Ohio and surrounding communities. The messaging emphasizes reliability, responsiveness, and the station’s role in keeping listeners informed when normal routines and communication channels were disrupted.

As part of WMRN’s broader emergency coverage branding, this announcement illustrates how the station positioned itself as essential listening during developing local situations.
