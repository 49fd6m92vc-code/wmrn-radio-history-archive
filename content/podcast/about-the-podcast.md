The WMRN Radio History Archive Podcast serves as the official audio companion to the WMRN Radio History Archive.

This introductory episode explains the purpose of the project, the importance of preserving broadcast history in Marion, Ohio, and how recorded audio, personal memories, and archival materials contribute to a more complete understanding of WMRN’s legacy.

---

## Episode Overview

This episode outlines:

- The origins of the WMRN Radio History Archive  
- The importance of preserving local broadcast history  
- The role of oral history and recorded sound in archival work  
- How listeners can contribute recordings, photographs, and personal recollections  

The podcast is designed not simply as entertainment, but as a documented extension of the archive itself.

---

## Archival Context

The WMRN Radio History Archive Podcast presents historical recordings, contextual analysis, and interpretive commentary related to WMRN and its role in the Marion community.

Episodes may include:

- Preserved broadcast recordings  
- Commentary on significant historical moments  
- Interviews and oral histories  
- Documentation of longstanding community traditions  

Each episode forms part of the growing digital record of WMRN’s history.
