# THE WMRN ARCHIVES: “Power Outage” - Emergency Coverage Promo, WMRN News

This episode presents a preserved WMRN News promotional announcement titled “Power Outage.”

The promo highlights WMRN’s role as a critical source of information during power outages affecting Marion, Ohio and surrounding communities. Emphasizing immediate updates, service interruptions, and local impact, the piece reinforces the station’s identity as essential listening when normal routines were disrupted.

As part of WMRN’s broader emergency and public service coverage, the announcement reflects how the station positioned itself as a dependable resource during local disruptions and developing situations.
